Story of VIBS

Vijaya Innovative Business Solutions was founded on September 7, 2014 in Mangalore, Karnataka. Since past 4 years we have established as a leading end to end solution provider for Design, Development and Digital Marketing Services. Our portfolio speaks for itself. We have successfully and timely completed several projects for our clients. This has helped to position VIBS, as a market leader for Design, Development and Digital Marketing services in and around Mangalore.

We believe in setting ambitious goals for our Company and work hard to achieve them. Since 2016, we have started working with foreign clients from countries such as USA, Canada, UK, Australia and Middle East Countries. This has helped us to get international exposure and considerably raise our working standards.

Design, Development, Digtal Marketing - 3 D's of VIBS.

In today's digital era, as the consumers go online, it has become imperative for a modern business to have digital presence. A successful business needs to target, personalize, engage, retain and re-target consumers.

Our 3 main verticals are Design, Development and Digital Marketing. These 3 verticals are in perfect harmony and conceived in a right way to scale up your business from Conception to Implementation. Our integrated solutions help organization with customized solutions, leverage cross channel integration to engage your consumers, access business outocomes through performance indicators and provide end to end solutions for your company.
